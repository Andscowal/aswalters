Front End Development

Organized and semantic HTML5 and CSS3 to bring your designs to life. I prefer coding from scratch instead of using a framework to ensure there’s no junk code or bugs.

Responsive Design

No need to worry about how your website looks across devices, I specialize in bringing full desktop sites down to the smallest mobile device, and everything in between. Consistency from your PSDs to beautiful usable mobile layouts is key.

User Experience

Great websites are defined by a great user experience. I take UX seriously, combining beautiful design with data-driven research to direct user flow. This approach produces beautiful products that are attractive, functional and intuitive for the user.

WordPress Development

All WordPress websites are built to bring you the custom design and feel you're looking for, along with the admin you need to edit all content, as well as specializing in custom PHP functions.

WooCommerce Development

I’ve worked with many eCommerce websites and have found WooCommerce to be a fantastic add-on to WordPress for your store. I can help customize your cart pages, as well as guide you to the best plugins available for your needs.

Accessibility and Semantics

Accessibility and semantics are important to code standards. Descriptive but short classes and well-commented code allow for easy revisions later on. Proper use of alt tags and labels ensure users with assistive devices can easily use your website, too.
